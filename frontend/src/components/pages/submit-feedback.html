Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit feedback anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to submit your feedback anonymously. However, we won't be able to follow up with you directly.

Who reviews my feedback?

The relevant department heads and appointed administrators review all submissions.

Is my feedback confidential?

Yes, all feedback is treated confidentially and is only shared with relevant personnel for resolution.

Response Time?

We aim to respond to all matters and give feedback within 48 hours.

How do I track my feedback?

Use the unique Tracking ID provided after submission on the "My Feedback" page.

Who can use this portal?

All SEKU students, staff members, and external visitors are welcome to share their experiences.
