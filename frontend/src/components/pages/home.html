Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit feedback anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to submit your feedback anonymously. However, we won't be able to follow up with you directly.

Who reviews my feedback?

The Director of Human Resources and relevant department heads.

Is my feedback confidential?

Yes, all feedback is treated confidentially and is only shared with relevant personnel for resolution purposes.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all feedback within 3-5 business days. Urgent matters are addressed within 24 hours.
