Department Sections

"Strategic units designed to provide specialized ICT solutions and support across the university landscape."

ICT Support

This section is tasked with ensuring the availability of IT applications and user support administration across the University Community. They are the first point of contact for all technical inquiries.

System Development & Administration

This section handles core innovations, building custom university software, managing the ERP system, and ensuring seamless application implementation and rollouts.

System Security

Guarding the university's digital assets. This section manages End Point Security, Network Security (IPS/IDS), Threat Management, and secure VPN access.

Network and Infrastructure

The backbone of SEKU connectivity. They design, install, and maintain the university's physical network, server rooms, fiber optics, and high-performance computing environments.
